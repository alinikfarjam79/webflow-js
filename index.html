Design in Webflow

Build your store visually within the Webflow designer. Publish directly from Webflow and make any design change in no time.

Connect with Smootify

Connect your Webflow project to Shopify in just a few clicks with our official apps. No coding or manual conversions required.

Manage with Shopify

Keep always synced

You can easily import your Shopify data into Webflow and keep your Webflow project always synced with Shopify automatically.

01

Connect your store with Webflow

Pick the Webflow project you want to hook up to Shopify and let Smootify do its thing with one click. Before diving into customization, don't forget to connect your domain in the Smootify dashboard (in your project settings) and paste our magic JS script into the custom code section.

02

Launch the Shopify App into your store

Let's get Smootify set up! First, head over to the Shopify App Store and install the official Smootify app. Once it's installed, it'll automatically take you to the Smootify dashboard. There, you'll be able to link your Shopify store to your Webflow project. Easy peasy!

03

Customize the project with our appCustomize the project with our app

Customize any element of your store with the Official Smootify Webflow App. Copy & paste all the elements you need without moving out from the Webflow designer.

04

Keep CMSs synced with our server plan

You can easily import your Shopify data into Webflow and keep your Webflow project and Shopify store automatically synced through our CMS Server Sync functionality.
